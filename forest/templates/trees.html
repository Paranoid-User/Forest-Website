What trees exist in forests?


It varies on the climate of the forest.
Lets start on temperate forests.
Temperate forests can have big and small trees from the famous giant sequia to the giant oak.
Now tropical forests.
Tropical forests tallest trees emerge from the canopy e.g. kapoc and then right at the bottom the laurel tree.
Boreal forests get very cold and the trees are adapted to the snow allowing them to stand for the whole of the winter. Narrow small needles, waxy coating allows the tree to withstand the snow.

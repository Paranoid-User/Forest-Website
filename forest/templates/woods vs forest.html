So what is the difference between woods and forests?


Woods cover 25% to 60% of a tree canopy meanwhile forests cover 60% to 100% canopy

Forests have different three biomes: Temperate Forests (mild), tropical forests (hot and rainy) and boreal forests (cold).


Woods don't have biomes, are more focuses on Ancient woodland (UK) or Old growth land (USA).

Forests hold alot more life 80% Amphibian known species, 68% Mammals and 75% Bird species.

Woods generally hold land-dwelling animals such as deer, rabbit, hedgehogs.
